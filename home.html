LAMIA

Lamia was a queen of Libya who was loved by Zeus. Out of jealousy, Hera killed her children and cursed her with eternal torment. Transformed into a monster, Lamia became a devourer of children and a wandering spirit. Her pain and vengeance lived on, whispered through centuries as a symbol of loss, rage, and darkness.
